Who we're looking for

Caring people with the right qualifications

If you hold a Certificate III in Aged Care or you're a registered nurse and have a passion for providing top-quality care in the community, we're interested in talking to you about joining our dedicated and innovative team.

We work across the in-home and community support services sector — including brokered care in partnership with accredited providers in Bairnsdale — so every day is varied, local, and genuinely person-centred.

We carefully match each carer to clients by personality, medical needs and preferences. The carers who thrive with us tend to be:

  • Capable
  • Resilient
  • Motivated
  • Empathetic
  • Enthusiastic
  • Compassionate
  • Open-minded
  • Professional
  • Knowledgeable

What you'll need

✓ Certificate III in Aged Care, or registration as a nurse (RN) ✓ CPR and First Aid certification ✓ A genuine passion for community care

All our carers hold a minimum Certificate III in Aged/Community Care, plus CPR and First Aid. We are fully insured (public liability, professional indemnity and work cover).
