Qualified carers

Skilled, certified people in your home

All our carers hold a minimum Certificate III in Aged/Community Care, and every carer is current in both CPR and First Aid. It means the person who walks through your door has the training to support you safely and confidently.

Beyond their qualifications, we look for the human qualities that make care feel like care. Our carers are:

  • Capable
  • Resilient
  • Motivated
  • Determined
  • Joyful
  • Empathetic
  • Enthusiastic
  • Compassionate
  • Open-minded
  • Professional
  • Experienced
  • Contemporary
  • Knowledgeable

Fully insured

Cover that protects everyone

Inner Peace is fully insured, so care is delivered in a secure environment for you, your family and our carers alike. Our cover includes:

Public liability

Protection for incidents that may occur while care is provided in your home or out in the community.

Professional indemnity

Cover for the professional, clinical and personal care services we deliver.

Work cover

Our carers are properly covered while they work, so you never carry that responsibility.

Personalised carer matching

The right carer, matched to you

Care is personal, so the relationship should be too. We carefully select your carer based on personality, the medical attention required, and your own specific preferences.

The result is a familiar, trusted face — someone you genuinely look forward to seeing, who understands both your needs and the way you like things done.

RN-led, local, since 2022

Founded and led by a registered nurse

Inner Peace was founded in 2022 by Annette Keat, a Registered Nurse with more than 30 years of nursing experience — including 12 years in home-based nursing and a Graduate Certificate in Palliative Care. We're locally owned and proud to care for our own East Gippsland community.
