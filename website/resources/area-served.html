Towns we cover across East Gippsland

Bairnsdale

The regional hub of East Gippsland — our home base. We provide the full range of personal, nursing, social and dementia care to Bairnsdale homes, and broker services in partnership with accredited local providers.

Book in Bairnsdale →

Bruthen

A close-knit rural town north-east of Bairnsdale. We bring reliable in-home care to Bruthen so residents can stay connected to their community and country surroundings.

Book in Bruthen →

East Gippsland

From the lakes to the hills, we serve the wider East Gippsland region — the locally owned, RN-led service that has cared for this community since 2022.

Book across East Gippsland →

Lakes Entrance

Our coastal community by the Gippsland Lakes. We support Lakes Entrance clients with personalised home care that fits the relaxed, waterside pace of life.

Book in Lakes Entrance →

Lindenow South

A small farming community in the Mitchell River valley. Distance is no barrier — we travel to Lindenow South so rural residents receive the same quality care as everyone else.

Book in Lindenow South →

Paynesville

Our boating village on the edge of the lakes. We help Paynesville residents keep living independently at home, surrounded by the place and people they love.

Book in Paynesville →
