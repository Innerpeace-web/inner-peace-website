Employee Handbook

Welcome to Inner Peace In-Home Nursing & Care. This handbook outlines our philosophy of care and the standards we uphold as a premium provider in East Gippsland.

1. Our Philosophy of Care

Inner Peace was founded on the belief that clinical excellence must be paired with genuine human connection. As a boutique, RN-led service, we don't just complete tasks; we build relationships. When you enter a client's home, you are a guest representing our core values of dignity, respect, and clinical precision.

2. Navigating Rural Healthcare

Providing care across East Gippsland presents unique challenges. You will often travel long distances between Bairnsdale, Lakes Entrance, and remote properties.

  • Communication Blackspots: Always download your ShiftCare roster offline before leaving town.
  • Vehicle Safety: It is your responsibility to ensure your vehicle is serviced, insured, and contains an emergency kit.
  • Time Management: Travel time is calculated into your roster. Do not rush on rural roads.

3. Professional Boundaries

Because we care deeply, lines can sometimes blur. It is vital to maintain professional boundaries to protect both you and the client. Do not accept large gifts, do not share personal financial information, and always escalate complex emotional situations to the RN Coordinator.
