Welcome to the Team

Your First Week

Onboarding Checklist

We are thrilled to have you join Inner Peace. To ensure you are fully compliant and confident before your first solo shift, please complete all items below.

  • 1. Signed Employment Contract

    Returned to Admin via email.

  • 2. Banking & Superannuation Details

    Uploaded to Xero Me profile.

  • 3. Compliance Uploads

    Please upload your NDIS Worker Screening Check, National Police Check, and AHPRA Registration (if RN) to the portal.

  • 4. Mandatory Training Modules

    Complete Infection Control and Manual Handling in the Training Hub.

  • 5. Shadow Shift with Senior RN

    Scheduled for Thursday. You will accompany Annette to 3 client visits.

Next Steps

Once your compliance documents are verified, you will receive your ShiftCare login and your initial roster.

Don't forget to stop by the Bairnsdale office to pick up your PPE kit, name badge, and uniform!
