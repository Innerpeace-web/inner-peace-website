More than care

We create opportunities that enrich lives

Ever since 2022, Inner Peace In-Home Nursing & Care has been committed to providing high-quality, personalised care that promotes well-being and health for people across East Gippsland, Victoria.

Our approach goes beyond the confines of traditional residential care — empowering individuals and their families to embrace a fulfilling life within the comfort of their home.

We believe in cherishing life's simple pleasures, whether it's a refreshing day at the local pool, hosting a social gathering, or exploring hidden treasures at the op shop. Through it all, we make sure that we create opportunities that enrich lives.

Meet our founder

About Annette Keat

Inner Peace was founded by Annette Keat, a Registered Nurse with more than 30 years of nursing experience, including 12 years dedicated to home-based nursing. In 2020 she completed a Graduate Certificate in Palliative Care with the Australian College of Nursing.

Across three decades of caring for others, one belief has stayed at the centre of everything Annette does: "I want to ensure that people always remain at the heart of care."

It's this conviction that shaped Inner Peace — a service built to give people more care, more respect, more options, and the opportunity to live life to the fullest, in the place they love most: home.

Annette's credentials

  • 12 years dedicated to home-based nursing
