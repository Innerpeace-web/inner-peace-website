Dementia Care

Dementia Nurse Consulting — specialist support for dementia care. Compassionate, personalised support for people living with dementia, their families, carers, and care providers across East Gippsland.

Clear, practical dementia support with a human centre

Working alongside individuals, families, carers, and providers, we deliver person-centred dementia support that promotes dignity, safety, wellbeing, and meaningful connection to home and community.

  • Person-centred
  • Compassionate
  • Practical
  • Aligned with aged care standards

Who we support

Support for everyone touched by dementia

  • People living with dementia at all stages
  • Support at Home (SaH) recipients
  • Families and carers
  • Home care recipients
  • Aged care and community providers

How we help

Practical, person-centred support

  • Personalised care planning
  • Family and carer guidance
  • Changed behaviour support and dementia strategies
  • Support for home care and aged care providers
  • Improving wellbeing, independence and quality of life

Specialist clinical services

Dementia expertise, where it’s needed most

Behaviour Assessment & Support

Understanding changed behaviours and practical strategies to respond with confidence.

Individualised Care Planning

Tailored plans built around the person, their history and their goals.

Family & Carer Support

Guidance, education and reassurance for the people providing day-to-day care.

Provider & Workforce Consultation

Specialist support for home care and aged care providers and their teams.

Quality of Life Strategies

Practical approaches that promote dignity, wellbeing and meaningful connection.

Assessment & Ongoing Support

Initial cognitive assessment, geriatric assessment, diagnosis support and ongoing support.
